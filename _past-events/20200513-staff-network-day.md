13th May is the National Staff Networks Day and as part of the Civil Service LGBT+ Network plans for the day we want to open up a discussion with network members to look at three things:

1. How has COVID-19 affected our community?
2. What are we doing that works for you?
3. Where would you like us to focus our efforts?

Places are limited so make sure you sign up and if you aren't able to attend let us know so we can free up space. You're welcome to join using your personal device if your department doesn't allow zoom, however we do need a gov.uk email address to send details of the event to.
