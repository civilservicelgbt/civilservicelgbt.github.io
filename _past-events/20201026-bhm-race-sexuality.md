### This event is being hosted by BEIS Faith and Minority Ethnic Network

Join us for a necessary dialogue to explore the intersectionality between race and sexual orientation, with a particular spotlight on racism, prejudice and discrimination within the LGBT community.

Speakers include:

• Rikesh Nagamah, Vice Chair, Civil Service LGBT+ Network

• David Seivwright, Food and Safety Agency

This event is open to all civil servants; please RSVP using your work email address. Participants will be sent the MS Teams joining link 2 working days before the event.

We look forward to seeing you there!

Please note, that this is will be a normal MS Team meeting and has a capacity of 250. We advise that you join the session promptly at the time stated in order to fully participate. Should you be unable to do join due to capacity issues, it is also possible to join this using the telephone details provided. Please remember to mute your mic, keep your camera off and raise your hand to ask a question or type it within the chat box.

After you have registered, the MS Teams Link will be sent two hours before the event.

Please follow us on Eventbrite to receive details of our other events. If you have any problems registering, please email famenetwork@beis.gov.uk
