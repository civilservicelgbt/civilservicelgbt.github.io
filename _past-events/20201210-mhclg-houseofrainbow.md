In celebration of World Aids Day, Ministry of Housing and Communities and Local Government [MHCLG] wonderful networks, MHCLGBT+ Network and MHCLG BAMENet will be co-hosting an event to mark the day. 

They have invited activist, author, theologian and the founder and CEO of House of Rainbow Rev. Jide Macaulay, which seeks to be a welcoming ministry for lesbians, gays, bisexuals, transgender, intersex, and queer (LGBTIQ) people, to speak to Civil Servants. 

Rev. Jide Macaulay will be discussing his personal inspiration and reflections on the work he does in the UK and Africa ministering to Christians and Muslims in Nigeria and Ghana, LGBTIQ communities and communities living with HIV and Aids. 

This session will be hosted on Microsoft Teams. Please sign up via the link above on Eventbrite. A link to join the event will be shared no later than two hours before the start of the event. Please add this event to your calendar in advance.
