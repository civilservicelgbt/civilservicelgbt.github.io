As we all adjust to life in lockdown, we are thinking of new ways to stay in touch with all our members. Following the success of our first social, we are going to make these more regular. As requested from attendees from the first session, we will use this time to get to know each other more with some questions.

## How do I join?

Please register your interest on Eventbrite using your work email and the email you will be joining the call on. You will be contacted with the Zoom joining details.

## Questions or ideas?

If you have any issues or questions about the social, or you have any other ideas on how we can do more for lesbian women, please email Leah Cunnah on info@civilservice.lgbt .

If you are unable to participate vocally, please let us know and we will make sure you can still join in safely. 

> Do you have a digital event coming up, or want to plan one? [Contact us](/about/contact-us/) and we can help you promote it.
