Following the success of our first digital social, we are back with a second one to see you into the May Bank Holiday weekend. 
 
Join us at 18:30, on Thursday 8th May (via Zoom) for the 5-point challenge. An interactive quiz game show that will make you think and hopefully laugh. 

Like last time, we will be using the Zoom feature of breakout sessions to facilitate virtual teams. Please register your interest via Eventbrite to receive further information. 

## Important to note: ## 

Registering your interest via Eventbrite does not guarantee your place. Your Zoom registration link will be sent to the email address you register your interest with. 

If you are unable to participate vocally, please let us know and we will make sure you can still join in safely.
