Hello! I'm Alex. I've been working in the Civil Service for about five years, and currently am a Workforce Planning Manager in the Department for Work and Pensions. I also am co-chair of DWP Pride, the LGBT+ Staff Inclusion Network, and have spent several years involved as part of the CS LGBT+ London Organising team. 

My role in CS LGBT+ SLT is focused on communications and engagement. We're very keen to grow network engagement across the Civil Service, so if you'd like to get involved or have any suggestions please do not hesitate to reach out.
