Hello! I’m Jeanie (jee-nee, she/they) and I work in the Planning Inspectorate (PINS), an arms-length body in the MHCLG family.  

I’m also the immediate past co-chair of the Rainbow Network at PINS. I’ve been a civil servant for over 20 years and a non-binary neurodivergent always. I’m a planner not lawyer, but I have a professional and personal interest in human rights and equality. I try to be sensational but am happiest doing a jigsaw in front of the telly!
