### From (MOD) DE&S Pride Network:

Issues of body image are particularly prevalent within the LGBT+ community. The Pride Network will be sharing their lived experiences of these issues, with tips on how all staff can promote a healthy body image in the workplace and beyond. The Pride Network hopes you will take away the following learning from the event:  
-	Develop an understanding of the impact that LGBT+ physical stereotypes can have upon the lives of those within the community. 
-	Gain an awareness of the physical and medical issues that are prevalent within and specific to the LGBT+ community. 
-	Increase your knowledge on how to promote positive body image and the impact this can have on colleagues, friends and family.
