As part of the PrideON month, the Civil Service LGBT+ network have organised a Bake Off, so that network members, and everyone in the LGBT+ & ally community across the Civil Service can join in.

Bakealong - Rainbow roses

You don’t have to be a show stopping baker, in this bake-a-long, we will be giving you some top tips for icing your cakes/ biscuits with icing rainbow roses (can be made by all ages). If you would like to join in, login at 20:00 and have the following things already prepared and ready to go: 

We will be making two types of roses. A butter cream version and a sugar paste version. 

For the buttercream version, you will need: 

A cupcake or biscuit
A piping bag
A star nozzle
Cling film
Butter cream in as many colours as you wish. For example, minimum of 4 for a rainbow, or you could make a non binary or trans flag. You could make your own buttercream or buy it ready made and just put colouring into it. 
Scissors 

For the sugarpaste rainbow rose, you will need: 

Ready rolled icing coloured in the colours you wish for your role
A food bag (see through plastic bag)
Knife

## How do I join?

Please register your interest on Eventbrite using the above link and your GOV.UK email. Our Zoom capacity is limited to 100 participants, so if you can no longer attend, please cancel your place. 

You may be required to register for Zoom access codes in advance of the event, so please keep an eye on your inbox. 

Any other instructions and materials needed for the event, will also be distributed to the email you register with in advance.
