The Joint Intelligence Organisation is a small unit at the heart of the UK’s National Security Community. It produces intelligence assessments that inform the Prime Minister, the National Security Council and the most senior policy makers on the security priorities of the day - often assessing the issues behind headlines. 

The JIO is on a mission to improve its diversity and inclusion. As part of this, they are keen to hold an Awareness Session to explain what it does; how it does it; and to bust a few myths about working in the National Security community.
