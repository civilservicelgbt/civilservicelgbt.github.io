### From the Cabinet Office: 

All civil servants, particularly GCF and CO staff, are invited to this event during LGBT+ History Month.

· Attendees will develop an understanding of sexual orientation, gender and gender identity and why it is essential to get the language right in communications

· Attendees will better understand what we need to do as individuals and as an organisation to ensure their full inclusion and equilibrium with other equality strands

· Explore the ethos of interrogating heteronormativity, encouraging intersectionality through the pedagogy of ‘usualising’

· Explore quick wins and long term strategies and resources for sustainable change

A link to the virtual event will be shared with people who have registered in advance.
