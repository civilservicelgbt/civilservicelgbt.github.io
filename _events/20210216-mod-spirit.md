### From (MOD) DE&S Pride Network:

The term ‘Spirit’ means different things to all of us. For some, the term brings religious connotations, whereas others may see term from a secular, humanist ‘community spirit’ angle. This session will explore both sides of the coin and what the term means to some of our members. Hayley Barnden, former Chair of the DE&S Pride Network, will also be joining us to share her experiences of being a Christian and LGBT: 
- Develop an understanding of the history and relationships between prominent religions, religious institutions and being LGBT+. 
- Gain an awareness of humanist and secular views of spirituality and what it means to be spiritual and LGBT+. 
- Increase your knowledge on how to promote a positive, holistic team spirit and the impact this can have upon your colleagues and staff.
