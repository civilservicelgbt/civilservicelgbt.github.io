UK Black Pride is the world’s largest celebration for LGBT+ people of African, Asian, Caribbean, Latin American and Middle Eastern-descent. It promotes unity and cooperation among LGBT+ people of diasporic communities in the UK, as well as their friends and families. For the third year in a row, it is being hosted in the Stratford Olympic Park and is a full day of performances, talks, community stalls and workshops.

If you would like to join other Civil Service colleagues in attending, please meet us from 11:00 in the Pret-a-Manger in Stratford Westfields – the one on the first floor, not the one on the lower ground floor! We will then make our way over to the Olympic Park from 12:00.

If you have questions about this event, or you have an idea for a future event, you can contact our [London Organisers](mailto:%20london@civilservice.lgbt).
