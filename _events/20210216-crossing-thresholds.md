### From Civil Service Race Forum:

The Civil Service Race Forum, has invited Thresholds to present a session to raise awareness of their courses they organise to support women

Over the last 18 years, Thresholds has established itself as a leading provider of highly effective, affordable career support for women.

Click on the link for further details about the Thresholds programmes - https://www.thresholds.co.uk/

One Day Workshop

One day workshop stimulates people to look at their careers with fresh eyes and supports them in defining the milestones to achieve their goals. Participants gain greater clarity of direction and confidence to pursue their aspirations at work and in life. Launching BAME courses in the new year, but the workshop can be delivered to BAME or mixed groups of women. Cost effective one day option. Further details and testimonials on the website.

12 Month Flagship programme

Their flagship year-long programme for women who want to develop their career in a structured and supportive environment.

The women they work with learn how to define what they want from their career and their life, and how to achieve it. This is foundational work; challenging, inspiring and stretching participants to achieve more than they could have expected. Mentoring partnerships are the cornerstone of the programme and enable participants to benefit from the experience and perspective of senior colleagues. Mixed groups of women. Further details and testimonials on their website.

Succeeding at Interviews

One day virtual workshop equips women of all professional backgrounds with the practical tools and self-belief to do themselves justice at interviews – no matter what questions they are asked. Participants will be shown how to identify and communicate their strengths most effectively, and structure their answers to convey their suitability for the position they want. Practice interviews and feedback from peers and facilitators helps people make the best possible impression when it comes to the real thing. They also offer a two day option. Mixed groups of women. Further details and testimonials on their website.
