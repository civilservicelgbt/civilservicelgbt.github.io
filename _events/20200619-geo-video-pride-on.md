## From the Goverment Equalities Office: 

Due to current circumstances this year's Pride events have taken to the online world for 2020. The theme is #YouMeUsWe - a call to show how important allyship is to the LGBT+ community. 

GEO are looking to create two videos that explore the theme of Allyship, to post on their Twitter. The questions they would like members to answer are:

1. What does allyship mean to you?
2. Why is it important a network of allies are available for LGBT people? 

The videos can be filmed on your smartphone, some good tips are:

- Film them in landscape
- Keep them to around about 30 - 60 seconds
- Try to find a nice background to include behind you if possible
- If you’re using sunlight please make sure that the sun is facing you rather than behind you to avoid a silhouette. 

Please send them to Hannah Toulson by Friday 19 June either by email to [hannah.toulson@geo.gov.uk](hannah.toulson@geo.gov.uk) or if the file size is too big we suggest using WeTransfer.
