### From (MOD) DE&S Pride Network:

In this session, Alexander Deering, Q+ Rep from the DE&S Pride Network will help introduce us to some key points around the topic of being transgender and share information on trans terminology, inclusivity tips and terminology:
-	To understand terminology used in reference to gender
-	Share some quick tips on what to say/not say in different scenarios
-	Share and signpost to resources on gender identity.
