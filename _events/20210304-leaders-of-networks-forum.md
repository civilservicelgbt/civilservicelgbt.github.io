The Leaders of Networks Forum is a regular event that helps shape the work of the Civil Service LGBT+ Network and give the leaders of Civil Service LGBT+ networks a space to share experiences, resources, ideas and solutions.

The event is open to anyone who helps to lead a Civil Service LGBT+ staff network (chair or co-chair equivalent) in a department, agency or arm's length body, or a local Civil Service LGBT+ Network itself.  

**To register your attendance, please sign up via Eventbrite using your work email**. 

The agenda for the event will be sent around one week in advance of the meeting. If you would like a specific item raised, please contact [us](mailto:info@civilservice.lgbt).
