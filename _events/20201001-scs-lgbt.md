Following the success of our previous event, the Cabinet Office LGBT+ Network has organised a cross-department panel discussion moderated by David Quarrey, the Prime Minister’s International Affairs Adviser and Deputy National Security Adviser! David is also the Cabinet Office's LGBT+ Senior Civil Service champion.

David will be joined by:
* Fiona Jones, Customer Experience Director, *Department for Work & Pensions*
* Shayne Scott, Deputy Director, Operations Financial Management, *Department for Education*
* Nathan Phillips, Director, Airports and Infrastructure, *Department for Transport*

The discussion will be an opportunity for panellists to share their thoughts, reflections and experiences relating to LGBT+ issues. You’ll also have the opportunity to engage with the panel throughout.

To make this event accessible to as many departments as possible, we will be hosting it using Microsoft Teams.

> To join the event, please email [LGBT@cabinetoffice.gov.uk](mailto:lgbt@cabinetoffice.gov.uk)
