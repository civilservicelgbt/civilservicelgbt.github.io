Are you a transgender, non-binary, gender fluid or genderqueer? Are you intersex, or have an intersex condition or variation in sex characteristics? 

Following the success of the first Cross Government Gender Identity Listening Circle, we will be hosting one a month during lockdown. 

Please join us for a virtual meeting facilitating a conversation about how Covid-19 and self-isolation have impacted you. The objective of this initiative is to support trans, non-binary and intersex staff across the Civil Service. 

If you don’t identify as trans, non-binary or intersex yourself, but you are in a position of supporting colleagues who are (through Diversity and Inclusion roles or as a line-manager), you are very welcome to join the conversation.  

The topic of this session will be ‘best practice from departments about inclusion of LGBT+ staff during the Covid-19 crisis. Contact keela.shackell-smith@cabinetoffice.gov.uk for the appointment.
