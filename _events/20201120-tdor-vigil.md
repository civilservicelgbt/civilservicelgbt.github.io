Join the Civil Service LGBT+ Network and a:gender at 12:00 on Friday 20th November, to remember and honour the lives of trans people whose lives have been lost in acts of anti-trans violence. 

Please sign up via Eventbrite with your GOV.UK email address. The event will be hosted on Zoom. If sign-ups exceed our Zoom capacity, we will look to use MS Teams or Google Meet. Joining details will be sent out on Friday morning.

This event is open to any Civil Servant.
