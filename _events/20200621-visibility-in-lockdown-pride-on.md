To mark Pride this year we’re celebrating LGBT+ visibility in lockdown through a photo/ video competition. 

## How to get involved? 

1. Pick a theme: 
Relationships and family (celebrating queer relationships and the different make up of families) 
Being Your True Self (non-stereotypical images of gender, masculinity and femininity) 
Queerness in a diverse world (celebrating those from BAME background, different socio-economic backgrounds, disabilities, etc) 
Love beyond the community (allies of the LGBT community celebrating pride, raising awareness, etc)

2. Choose your photo/video

3. Send to info@civilservice.lgbt by 21st June. 

4. We will pick run polls on our social media accounts w/c 22nd June to pick a winner for each category and one overall winner.
